## Changelog

### v0.1.0 - 2026-02-25

- **Project structure**: base layout for `data/`, `src/pipelines/`, `reports/figures/`.
- **Data loading**: simple script to download and save raw petitions CSV.
- **Data analysis**: basic descriptive stats and deeper research of signatures, durations, categories, and statuses.
- **Visualization**: scripts that generate figures for distributions, medians, activity by year, and petition outcomes.
- **Docs**: initial `README.md` and this `CHANGELOG.md` describing the lab workflow.
